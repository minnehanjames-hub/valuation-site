Valuation workbench

LBO, DCF, and Multiples

A small valuation lab for seeing how operating assumptions, leverage, discount rates, and exit multiples move enterprise value and sponsor returns.

Plain-English use case This is for learning the mechanics. It is intentionally simplified, but the outputs make the key value drivers visible.

LBO assumptions

Purchase to exit

Teaching model: assumes 55% of EBITDA after cash interest can repay debt. It still ignores taxes, capex, working capital, and fees.

About this project

Educational model

This workbench keeps the calculations intentionally visible. It is useful for understanding direction and sensitivity, not for producing a full investment banking model or investment recommendation.
